Instructions on how to play the game.

The objective of the game, is to collect all of the keys whithout getting caught by the minotaures. 

If keybinds are set to default:
	W = move up
	A = move left
	S = move down
	D = move left

	ALT + F = fog of war toggle
	CTRL + S = save
	CTRL + O = load
	CTRL + Q = exit

Another fun objective is, trying to clear the level in the least amount of time and least amount of steps.

Your score will be recorded in the highscores.
